Facilities Provided By Purohit School

Classroom

Spacious and air conditioned classrooms equipped with public address system, LCD Projector and white board provide all state of the art facilities and environment for Teaching - Learning process.

Library & Self reading Area

Purohit Science School provides isolated self-study tables for 11th and 12th Science students to focus during study hours. Our library is a treasure trove of knowledge, offering books on science, commerce, computers and general knowledge. Regular updates keep the collection fresh and engaging. The library is especially beneficial for secondary school students, keeping them informed and inspired.

Difficulty Solution Desk

Sometimes student feel fear during the lecture and he is not able to ask his query. So, to help students to come out of this fear and to get their queries solved, Purohit Science School has specially designed one system called Difficulty Solution Desks for the purpose of solution to each query. This would result in the growth and development of the students.

Councelling

Every student needs some personal help and guidance during his long journey to reach to the goal. In Purohit Science School, we have a permanent counsellor who serves these needs. Above all our subject experts also provide necessary counseling periodically to motivate the students.

Computer Lab

In today's world, computers are essential, and Purohit Science School ensures students grow with technology. Learning here is interactive, using CDs, LCD projectors, and multimedia tools. Our infrastructure includes 50+ computers. Our website keeps parents updated with results and news.
